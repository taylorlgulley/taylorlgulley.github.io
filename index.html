About Me

I studied Kinesiology at the University of Tennessee for my undergraduate education in order to pursue a Doctorate in Physical Therapy at Tennessee State University. I have always been fascinated by both Science and Mathematics. Learning about the human body and the physics behind it for the purposes of solving physical problems was very rewarding. However during a guest lecture about medical software systems I developed an interest in software development. This lead to me leaving the Doctorate program to later attend Nashville Software School in the pursuit of solving problems from a technology perspective.

Trivia-Brain
A trivia collection application allowing the user to manage their own categories and questions as well as test their knowledge. I made Trivia Brain for those wishing to test their trivia knowledge as well as to challenge my family during holiday gatherings. It was built with React for the Front-End and styled with Bootstrap/Reactstrap. I utilized an External API to populate default categories and a JSON Server for user stored data.
